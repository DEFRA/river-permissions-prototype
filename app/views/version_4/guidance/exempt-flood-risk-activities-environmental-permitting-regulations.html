Detail

Works that are carried out in or near a main river or sea defence are regulated under the environmental permitting regulations (EPR) as ‘flood risk activities’.

But you don’t need an environmental permit if you are doing one of the exempt activities listed in the EPR exempt flood risk activities PDF.

Check the PDF to find out if your activity is exempt and read the conditions you’d need to meet in order to operate without a permit.

To operate without a permit you must:

  • register the exempt activity with the Environment Agency
  • meet the description and conditions required for that exemption

If you want to apply for exemptions FRA21, FRA22, FRA23 or FRA24 you will also need to meet the dredging requirements.
