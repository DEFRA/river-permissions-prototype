Register your flood risk activity

Before you start

  • Read the flood risk activities guidance.
  • Decide which exemptions apply to your activities.
  • Check that each activity will meet the conditions of the relevant exemption.
  • Call us on 03708 506 506 to check if your activities are on a main river and if they are near any sensitive areas such as a nature reserve.

Registering takes around 15 minutes.

Start now

You'll need:

  • the grid reference where the work will happen
  • the name and address of the person or organisation responsible for the work
  • for a company, its registration number and registered address
  • for a partnership, name and address of all partners
  • contact details, if different to the person or organisation responsible
