Register a flood risk activity exemption

Before you register you must:

  • check you can meet the description and conditions for the exemption
  • have authority to register for an organisation or as an agent, if you are registering on their behalf

We will:

  • check if the location is environmentally sensitive or if it is too close to a flood defence structure
  • tell you whether we have registered the exemption within 10 working days

You will need:
  • the grid reference where the work will happen
  • the name and address of the organisation or person who is responsible for the work
  • for a company, its registration number and registered address
  • for a partnership, the name and address of all partners
  • contact details for the person we should contact about the work
