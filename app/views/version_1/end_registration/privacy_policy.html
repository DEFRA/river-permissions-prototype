Privacy policy: flood risk exemptions

The flood risk exemptions service is run by the Environment Agency. Information you give is handled in accordance with its personal information charter.

The personal information you give will be used to update the public register for flood activity exemptions.

This will include:

  • the name and address of the responsible organisation
  • the type of exempt activity you have registered
  • the locations of the activity

We may also use the personal information you give us to:

  • contact you about our customer service
  • send you environmental information, or consult you on environmental issues
  • carry out research on environmental issues
  • prevent someone from breaking environmental laws
  • investigate and take action when someone may have broken environmental laws
  • respond to freedom of information requests (if data protection law allows it)

We may pass your personal information to our agents or representatives so they can do any of these things on our behalf. And we may share your information with other organisations if the law requires it.
