Phone questions, but digital

Building

  • Are you building a new structure on, in, next to, or across a river?
  • What are you building?

Repair and maintain

  • Are you repairing a site or carrying out maintenance on, in, next to, or across a river?
  • What are you doing?

Animal welfare

  • Are you building a structure for animal welfare or cleaning gravel for fish spawning?
  • What are you doing?

Scaffolding and ladders

  • Are you erecting scaffolding or ladders in or over a river?
  • Will the structure be in place for longer than a day?

Floodplain digging

  • Are you digging in the floodplain?
  • What are you doing?

Cables

  • Are you crossing the river with cables or other services?
  • Are the cables or services crossing the river using an existing structure, such as a bridge?
  • Is the cable, service crossing inside or attached to a exisiting thing?
  • Are the cables or services being installed under the riverbed, in the river itself, or above the river?

Remove sand and silt

  • Are you removing sand or silt from the riverbed?
  • Are you dredging?
  • How long is the stretch of river you are dredging?
  • What are you doing?

Floodplain digging

  • Are you temporarily dewatering a work area?
