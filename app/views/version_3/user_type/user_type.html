Who is responsible for the activity?

How to decide who is responsible

The company, body or individual who has control over the way the activity (work) is done is responsible. They are called the ‘operator’. If you use contractors, you need to decide if it is you or the contractors who has control.

Whoever has control will usually:

  • have day-to-day control of the activity
  • make sure the activity complies with the exemption
  • decide who holds important staff positions and stop incompetent staff working, if needed
  • make financial decisions that affect the activity
  • take action to control the activity in an emergency
