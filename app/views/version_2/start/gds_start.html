Register your flood risk activity exemption

Before you start

  • Read the flood risk activities guidance.
  • Decide whether any of the exemptions apply to your activities.
  • Check that each activity will meet the conditions of the relevant exemption.
  • Call us to check if your activities are on a main river and if they are near any environmentally sensitive areas or a flood defence. Our number is 03708 506 506.

Dredging exemptions
You can’t register the exemptions for dredging, FRA23 and FRA24, online. Please contact us to register these.

It’s free to register an exemption. It should take about 10 minutes.

Start now

You'll need:

  • the grid reference where the work will happen
  • the name and address of the organisation or person who is legally responsible for the work
  • for a company, its registration number and registered address
  • for a partnership, the name and address of all partners
  • contact details for the person we should contact about the work
