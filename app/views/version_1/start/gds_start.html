River permissions

Before you start

You'll need to:

  • read the guidance on river permissions
  • decide which exemptions apply to your activities
  • check that the activity will meet the conditions of each relevant exemption you wish to register

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Start now

You'll need:

  • the national grid reference number for all locations where activites will be carried out
  • the name and address of the individual, business or organisation that will be carrying out the activities
  • the business registration number and registered address details if you’re registering exemptions for a company
  • the name and address details of all persons accountable if you're registering exemptions for a partnership
  • details of the person we should contact about the activities, and to whom we’ll send the exemption registration confirmation
